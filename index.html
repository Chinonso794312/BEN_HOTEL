Have Questions?

What time is check-in and check-out? +

Check-in time is 3:00 PM, and check-out time is 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out may be available for an additional fee upon request.

Is breakfast included in the room rate? +

Our standard rates do not include breakfast, but we offer several packages that do. Please check your booking details or contact the front desk for options.

Do you offer valet parking services? +

Yes, we provide valet parking for all guests at a rate of $35 per night. Self-parking options are also available nearby.

Are pets allowed at BEN_HOTEL? +

We welcome small dogs (under 25 lbs) in designated rooms for a one-time cleaning fee of $100. Please notify us at the time of booking.
